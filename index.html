The Dumping Problem

Health Hazards

Improper waste disposal creates breeding grounds for diseases and attracts pests that threaten community health.

Environmental Impact

Littering pollutes our soil, water sources, and air, destroying the natural beauty of our community.

Property Values

Areas with dumping problems see decreased property values and reduced quality of life for residents.

The Community Must Stop Littering!

Every piece of trash thrown on the ground is a step backward for our community's progress.

Our Solutions

Proper Waste Disposal

We should make sure that we throw papers and all waste into dustbins, not on the ground.

  • Use designated dustbins for different types of waste
  • Separate recyclables from general waste
  • Never throw waste on the street or in public spaces

Weekly Collection Service

Pick It Up waste collection service comes every week to collect dustbins so the place can be clean.

  • Reliable weekly collection schedule
  • Professional waste management service
  • Keeps our community consistently clean

Community Contribution

CWP (Community Work Programme) must contribute dustbins to the community for proper waste disposal.

  • Strategic placement of dustbins
  • Regular maintenance and replacement
  • Community-funded waste infrastructure

MANDELA DAY

JULY 18
MAKE A DIFFERENCE

"What counts in life is not the mere fact that we have lived. It is what difference we have made to the lives of others."

Today, on Mandela Day, we honor Nelson Mandela's legacy by taking action in our community. Our fight against dumping and littering is more than just keeping our environment clean - it's about making a lasting difference in the lives of everyone who calls this place home.

Just as Mandela believed in the power of collective action, we believe that together, we can transform our community one piece of trash at a time, one dustbin at a time, one act of service at a time.
